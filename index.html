About Us

“PRAYOG by IIT Bombay” is a new initiative started by CTARA, IIT Bombay since June 2021 to connect with society and address societal problems. PRAYOG is a secretariat which will provide solutions to societal problems using multiple mechanisms, some existing and some new. It is a platform to bring solution seekers and solution providers together while engaging with CSRs as well as to provide various avenues for CTARA graduates. There are many problems in our country which need to be addressed and need inputs from various stakeholders including academia. There is also a major role that academia can play in addressing societal processes through training, capacity building and applied research.
